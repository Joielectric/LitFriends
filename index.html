About Us

Consenting Adults is a collaborative group of independent audio artists who believe the best work happens together. We pair voices, skills, and visions — writers with producers, performers with storytellers — to create intimate audio that neither could build alone.

Every piece we release is crafted with intention: high production quality, thoughtful performance, and a genuine desire to realize each artist's creative vision. We're always open to new collaborators. If you have a voice, a story, or a skill that belongs here — reach out.
