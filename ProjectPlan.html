Lit Friends Collective

Audio Production
Project Plan

A 4-week collaborative framework for writers, voice actors, editors, and sound designers.

Week 1

Scoping & Vision

Choose the project and define its boundaries

Week 2

Casting & Recording

Assign roles and capture all performances

Week 3

Editing

Clean, arrange, and sync all audio

Week 4

Post-Production

Polish, master, and publish the final piece

Week 1

Scoping & Vision

Creative

Pitch and vote on the project concept
Define tone, genre, and target runtime
Identify all characters and voice roles needed
Finalize the script or narrative outline

Logistics

Set the full project timeline and milestones
Confirm team availability and capacity
Create shared folder and file naming conventions
Agree on delivery platform and audio format
Deliverable Approved script + project brief shared with all collaborators
Week 2

Casting & Recording

Casting

Match voice actors to roles based on fit and availability
Send out character briefs and script sections
Confirm audio specs (sample rate, format, mic proximity)

Recording

VAs record and submit raw takes by mid-week
Review submissions and flag retakes needed
Collect retakes and confirm all parts received
Hand off all audio files to the editor
Deliverable All approved raw recordings submitted and organized in the shared folder
Week 3

Editing

Cleanup

Noise reduction and breath trimming on all tracks
Normalize levels across all voice files
Remove false starts, long pauses, and artifacts

Assembly

Arrange all tracks in the project timeline
Sync dialogue, pacing, and emotional beats
Layer in placeholder SFX and ambient beds
Share rough cut for team review and notes
Deliverable Approved rough cut handed off to post-production
Week 4

Post-Production

Finishing

Mix all elements — voice, music, and SFX
EQ, compression, and spatial treatment per track
Master to target loudness (-16 LUFS for streaming)
Export final file in all required formats

Publishing

Write title, description, and tags for platforms
Create cover art or post visual if needed
Upload and schedule the release
Share to community channels and credit all collaborators
Deliverable Published audio — live, credited, and shared with Lit Friends
