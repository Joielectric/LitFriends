About

Wellnobodysperfect is a voice artist who defies a single label — and that's exactly the point. She's funny and self-aware, bubbly and a little chaotic, with a very switchy style that keeps things delightfully unpredictable. One moment tender, the next a complete brat. Usually both at once.

She loves exploring new territory, diving into creative challenges, and bringing a genuine sense of play to everything she makes. If you're looking for something a little unexpected, a little messy, and a lot of fun — you're in the right place.

"Perfect is boring. Messy is fun!"

— Wellnobodysperfect
