About

Filthy Bunny is a voice artist, writer, and storyteller who defies the expected. Versatile, creative, and utterly uninhibited in her approach to sexuality, she brings her characters to life through nuance, vulnerability, and warmth.

Whether she's playing the tender caretaker or the playful sub who knows exactly what she wants, there's an honesty to everything she creates. She doesn't perform — she embodies.

Self Love is Self Care.

— Filthy Bunny
