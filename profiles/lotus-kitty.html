About

Lotus Kitty came here to explore — sexuality in all its forms, yours and hers and everyone's. Friendly by nature and introverted by design, she prefers to experience online intimacy through art, audio, and writing. She's a listener, a feeler, a quiet celebrant of the erotic. The wilder side she reveals to few can be glimpsed, winking playfully, in her art.

Happily poly partnered and unapologetically herself: demisexual, kinky, an exhibitionist with a love of bondage and intense sensation. She brings that same depth of feeling to everything she creates — thoughtful, original, evocative, and wholly her own.
