About

Loona Licks is a voice artist and audio erotica creator with a gift for intimacy, imagination, and genuine connection. Warm, playful, and endlessly creative, she brings fantasy to life through sound — always shaped by one delicious question: what do you want to hear?

Deeply collaborative by nature, Loona thrives when talented people come together to make something special. She is currently on hiatus — but her work remains, and she'll be back when the moment is right.

I absolutely love hearing all of your delicious desires."

Who would you like me to be? What do you want to hear?
