About

MissKittenSK has been a cornerstone of the erotic audio space for over 10 years. A master of improvisation with a wide and fearless range, she built one of the most beloved back catalogues in the community — 420+ audios spanning every mood, fantasy, and desire.

Known for her warmth, wit, and total commitment to the craft, Kitten forged deep connections with her audience through genuine intimacy and endless variety. From tender girlfriend experiences to unhinged improv, she did it all — and she did it with style.

Now retired from recording, she continues to contribute to the community through script writing and occasional collaborations with the Consenting Adults collective and JOI Electric.
